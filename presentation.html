Redesign overview

Professional presentation,
cleaner story

The goal was not just cosmetics. We aligned the new website back to the motadata/aligned, client-facing narrative while removing outdated certification claims and keeping the visual system more polished.

No hardcoded secrets

Removed any PAT/token-style onboarding artifacts from the marketing narrative and kept all integration references conceptual.

Faster, clearer presentation

This deck documents what changed, what matured, and what got removed so stakeholders can compare old vs. new quickly.

Design system

Color palette

Light surfaces with deep navy text, crimson accents, and restrained glass effects.

Light mode Neutral warmth Motion with restraint

Identified improvements

  • Readability: moved from low-contrast dark gradients to clean typography rhythm.
  • Hover density: removed excessive glow surfaces and high-contrast glass noise.
  • Trust messaging: replaced certification lead ins with client outcomes and services.
  • Consistency: crimson + warm steel instead of multiple competing accent hues.
  • No certification claims: removed ISO 27001, SOC 2, GDPR, and trust-badge wording.

Interactive behaviors

  • Typewriter — clean single-line cycling with softer cursor rhythm.
  • Accordions — solutions details without forcing the visitor to read everything immediately.
  • Carousel — testimonial track with dot state and pause-on-hover behavior.
  • Form validation — client-side checks, error states, and rate-limit feedback.
Takeaways
